Privacy Policy – Workout App (Private Family Use)

Last updated: 2025-09-23

1. Controller & Contact

Controller: Maximilian Weuthen
Contact: m.weuth@web.de

2. Personal Data We Process

  • Account data: email (Supabase authentication).
  • Profile data: display_name, avatar_url (profile picture).
  • Workout data: program/type, week/unit, duration/time, reps, notes, progress, timestamps.
  • Uploads (optional): photos/screenshots you attach to workouts.
  • Device permissions: camera and photo library are requested only to take or select photos for uploads.

3. Purposes & Legal Bases

  • Account & sign-in; store and show your workouts – performance of a contract (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR).
  • Family compare/leaderboard view (showing display name, avatar and aggregated progress to other family members you share with) – legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR). Our interest: enable voluntary comparison and motivation within your family group. You can opt out by not using the feature or by removing your profile data.
  • Photo/Screenshot uploads – consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR). You can withdraw consent anytime by deleting the uploads.

4. Storage & Processing

We use Supabase (managed Postgres, authentication, and object storage) as our processor.
Region: eu-central-1 (Frankfurt).
Data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest by our provider. Access is restricted to authenticated users and protected by role-based rules.

5. Sharing & Visibility

We do not sell data and do not share it with third parties for their own purposes. Within the app, your display name, avatar and aggregated progress may be visible to other signed-in members of your private family group in the Compare view. All other workout details remain private to your account.

6. International Transfers

If processing involves subprocessors outside the EEA, transfers rely on Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) and additional safeguards. Details available on request.

7. Retention & Deletion

We keep data while your account exists. You can start deletion in-app: Settings → Delete account.
When you request deletion, your account is scheduled for deletion after 30 days. If you sign in again within 30 days, the deletion is cancelled. After 30 days without sign-in, the account and associated data are deleted.

8. Your Rights (GDPR)

You have the right to access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, and objection. You can lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority. To exercise rights, email m.weuth@web.de or use the in-app settings.

9. Children

The app is intended for private family use. If minors use the app, a parent/guardian should manage the account. We do not process special categories of data.

10. Security

We apply technical and organizational measures including TLS, access controls, least-privilege roles, and encrypted storage by our provider. We do not use advertising or analytics SDKs.

11. Changes

We may update this policy. The latest version is available on this page.
